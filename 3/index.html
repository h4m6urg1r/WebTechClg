Dictionary

Sonder
The realization that each passerby has a life as vivid and complex as your own.
Epiphany
A sudden and profound realization or understanding, often triggered by a mundane or everyday experience.
Serendipity
The occurrence of fortunate events by chance or accident, leading to unexpected and pleasant discoveries.
Ephemeral
Lasting for only a short period of time; transient or fleeting.
Saudade
A deep emotional state of longing or nostalgia for something or someone absent, often accompanied by a sense of incompleteness or melancholy.
Panacea
A remedy or solution that is believed to cure all ailments or problems.
Quixotic
Characterized by lofty, romantic, or idealistic pursuits or ambitions that are often impractical or unrealistic.
Eunoia
Beautiful thinking; a state of well-mind and good spirit.
Petrichor
The pleasant, earthy smell that follows a rain shower, particularly after a dry spell.
Halcyon
Denoting a period of peace, tranquility, and prosperity; often associated with nostalgic or idyllic memories.
